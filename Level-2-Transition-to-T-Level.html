Level 2 Transition to T-level

The level 2 course I am studying is designed as a stepping stone onto the college's level 3 Digital production design and development T-level. The target for the level 2 course is for those who were unable to obtain a grade 4 in both GCSE maths and english at their respetive secondary schools.

There are 4 units which are covered on the Transition course, and they are;

  1. Creating Digital Graphics
  2. Creating a digital Portfolio (HTML)
  3. Computer Networks
  4. The online world
