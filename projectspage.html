As a developer for both HTML and the Apple platform, I follow a design workflow that is adaptable and effective. Here's an overview:

Rapid Application Design and Development (RAD)

RAD focuses on ongoing development and customer feedback rather than strict design plans. It consists of four key stages:

  1. Requirement Gathering: This stage involves gathering client requirements to ensure alignment with their needs and expectations.
  2. Rapid Prototyping: Teams collaborate to create working prototypes, often focusing on UI design.
  3. Construction and Deployment: Based on user feedback, the prototype is refined and deployed, ready for distribution.
