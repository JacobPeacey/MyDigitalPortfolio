My design and development workflow


As i develop for both HTML and the Apple platform. I use a design workflow process which i know works well with both. The process i use works great until im given a brief, or strict design documentaation i need to stick to.


Rapid Application Design and development


I use a form of workflow called Rapid applcation design and development. RAD focuses more on ongoing development and developing offthe bnack of customer feedback, as opposed to strict design plans and wireframes.


The 4 stages of RAD


Requirement Gathering

The requirement gathering stage of RAD involves liasing with clients, making attemps to build a picture as to what the client actually wants out of the job. In my Opinion, requirement gathering is the most significant element of the workflow. I think this becuase if you dont have the right idea of what the client wants, you run the risk of developing a poor product which leaves the client with a bad reputation of the company. This in the long run may harm your client relationship or business as a whole


Rapid Prototyping

The Rapid prototyping stage of Raid application development involves teams working together to create a working prototype model of the product, usually the UI in the mobile app development Space.

Construction and deployment

Construction and deployment is essentially an extension of the RP stage. Construction and deployment involves those same teams taking the user feedback that they recieved from the Rapid prototyping stage and improving that beta product into a functioning working product. Deployment is the stage where the product or service is then made avalliable to the customer, or made distributable on a public platfor. In my case, the iOS app Store.
