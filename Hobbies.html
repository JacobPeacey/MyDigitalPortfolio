My Inspiration

Who do I look up to, you may ask? The answer you would receive is Steve Jobs. The answer as to why? There's many. If I had to give you one, it would have to be how Steve came from what you could call a disadvantaged background. Despite that, he did what he loved, and sometimes what he didn't (That Calligraphy class in college). Despite the fact he didn't particularly enjoy that course, little did everyone know that Calligraphy skills would later form the screen savers of the Mac's we know and use today. It's not just Steve that I look up to; it's his inventions and his visions too. Specifically, Apple. Some people call me obsessed with Apple's hardware and software, which to be honest, I can't deny. So, knowing that, I thought. How can I put this so-called obsession to good use.
