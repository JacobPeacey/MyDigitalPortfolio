Jacob's College Journey

Hey, I'm Jacob. Currently, I'm immersed in a college course at Bournemouth and Poole College, stationed at the Landsdowne site—one of the three sites the college boasts. I proudly belong to the college's Digital and Creative Industries curriculum area. My fascination with computers dates back to my early years, around age 5. I've always been captivated by the intricate workings of computers, be it hardware, software, or the language that modern-day computers operate on. This profound interest enables me to channel an intense focus on anything IT-related. Admittedly, this focus sometimes overshadows other facets of my education. I found myself consistently choosing IT-related pursuits over what others deemed more crucial (though I must assert my disagreement with that widely held perspective). Regrettably, this arguably skewed prioritization led to falling behind on my schoolwork. It wasn't until I realized the gravity of my situation that panic set in. Truth be told, despite everyone urging me to revise more, I was at a loss, as I didn't know where to begin.
